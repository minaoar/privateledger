About PrivateLedger

Privacy-First Personal Expense Tracker

PrivateLedger is a local-only personal finance application that helps you understand your monthly spending across multiple bank accounts and credit cards without giving any third-party access to your financial data.

PrivateLedger uses your downloaded bank transactions files locally and generates meaningful spending insights while keeping all data on your own machine.

No cloud. No bank credentials. No data leaving your system.
Key Features
  • 100% local - all your financial data stays on your machine
  • Import transactions from OFX/QFX files (supported by most banks)
  • Automatic transaction categorization with pattern matching
  • Multi-account support for comprehensive spending insights
  • Monthly spending trends and category breakdowns dashboard
