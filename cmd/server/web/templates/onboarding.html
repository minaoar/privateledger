Your Privacy-First Finance Manager

PrivateLedger is a local-only personal finance application that helps you understand your monthly spending across multiple bank accounts and credit cards - without giving any third party access to your financial data. PrivateLedger keeps all your financial data on your computer. No cloud sync, no third parties, just you and your data.
