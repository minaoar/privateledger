How to Download Transactions from Your Bank

Most banks in North America let you download your bank transaction history in OFX (Microsoft Money) or QFX (Intuit Quicken) format. PrivateLedger uses those transaction history files to build a local database on your machine.

Privacy First: All your financial data stays on your machine. PrivateLedger never sends your data to the cloud.

Other Banks

For other banks, look for options like:

  • Download or Export in your account statements section
  • Choose OFX (Microsoft Money) or QFX (Intuit Quicken) format
  • Select the date range you want to download
  • Most US and UK banks support these formats
